Why La Lumiere?

Because everything we do is founded on relationships. From the lakeside dorms and close-knit classrooms to the fireplace in our fabled Moore House, our idyllic campus provides a setting like no other. But our most reliable and powerful asset is a culture where buying in is just what you do.

Our Favorite Spots on Campus

Lakeside Dorms

Whether your dorm sits on Lake La Lumiere or not, theres bound to be a beautiful view of water, forest, or architecture nearby. One thing is certain: nature abounds on campus and theres no shortage of beauty to dwell on.

Lake La Lumiere

What do rowing crew, cannonballing, making bonfires, sunbathing, kayaking, and fishing have in common at La Lumiere? They all happen in and around our lake.

Moore House

The foundational edifice of La Lumiere once served as a library, classrooms, dining hall, dorm, and officesall at the same time. These days its where we eat our meals and hang out together.

Sullivan Student Activities Center

The SAC (also known as the Sullivan Center) is a place to go when youre looking to relax and spend some down time. Students can play pool and table tennis, and its also been known to host video game tournaments and live music performances. Additionally, the SAC houses our Campus Store, where anyone can go to buy snacks, basic toiletries, and La Lumiere gear.

The Quad

One of the first spots you see when you step foot on campus, the Quad is an excellent place to study, chat, or play lawn games (the campus favorite is Spikeball, pictured here). Its not uncommon to see classes taught on the Quad on nice days.

The Chapel

One of La Lumieres older buildings, the Chapel once served as the center for spiritual and religious life on campus. While no longer functional, it still serves as a beautiful reminder of where we came from.

Marsch Gymnasium

Marsch Gym, with its iconic half-pipe shape, was designed by renowned modernist architect Helmut Jahn in the late 1970s. It serves as our primary gymnasium, where all indoor athletic and large-scale public events take place.

The Amphitheater

The Amphitheater is truly the hidden gem of La Lumieres campus. Tucked back into the woods and set on the lake, its a place students and faculty can visit privately for repose and reflection, but also sometimes serves as a gathering place for classes, Mass, and the Senior Candles tradition.

Scenic Trails

Trails for hiking and running can be found throughout our campus, weaving through woods and open fields and alongside our two lakes.

The Academic Building

This is where the bulk of the school day takes place. The Academic Building is home to a vast majority of our classrooms, and even has a dormitory adjoined to it. Up top on the Academic Building benches is where Lakers can be found chatting and studying at all hours of the day.

The Stairway to Success

This iconic spot on La Lumieres campus connects the upper campus with the lower campus and dorms, and comes complete with excellent views in all directions.
